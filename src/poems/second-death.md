<div class="stanza">They spend decades<br>guarding their breath.<br>Of course, they call it a tragedy—<br>the old poets told them it is.<br>I sat for my own wake at twenty-four.<br>Woke up in their hell.<br>A Friday. A morning.<br>My heaven.<br>How tragic<br>to only die once.<br>What a shame<br>to never not follow the script<br>they hand you.<br>I won't gasp when it happens<br>for the second time.<br>It will just be a Friday,<br>all over again.</div>
