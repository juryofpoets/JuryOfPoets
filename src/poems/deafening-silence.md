<div class="stanza">Haunting screeches, ever so loud,<br>always dissonant—<br>two people inside one<br>until I was twenty-four.<br>I then did some right things,<br>though none of them felt like it.<br>Now, here I am.<br>Sitting on this chair,<br>contemplating alone<br>how deafening this silence is.<br><em>This is for the better,</em> I tell myself,<br>and wait for him to agree.<br>But for once—<br>he is quiet.<br>Finally. <em>finally.</em></div>
