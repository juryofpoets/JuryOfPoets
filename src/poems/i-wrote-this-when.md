<div class="stanza">I made a mistake.</div>

<div class="stanza">You.</div>

<div class="stanza">
I wrote what my heart wanted.<br>
It started with "You."<br>
It was me. It was mine.
</div>

<div class="stanza">It was too much for you.</div>

<div class="stanza">
I changed a word.<br>
Then a line.<br>
Then the reason for the line.
</div>

<div class="stanza">
I rewrote it again,<br>
and again,<br>
and again.
</div>

<div class="stanza">
Fifty-seven versions<br>
you didn't get to read.
</div>

<div class="stanza">
Fifty-seven versions<br>
I used to be.
</div>

<div class="stanza">
This is the one<br>
that stayed.
</div>

<div class="stanza">I finally corrected my mistake.</div>

<div class="stanza">
I made a mistake,<br>
<em>again.</em>
</div>

<div class="stanza">
This is not—<br>
Me.
</div>
